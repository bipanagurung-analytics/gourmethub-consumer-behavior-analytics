## Executive Summary

GourmetHub is a specialty food retailer operating through web, catalog, and physical store channels. Despite collecting rich customer data, marketing decisions were largely based on intuition rather than evidence.

This project develops an end-to-end analytics pipeline that transforms customer data into actionable business insights. Using customer segmentation, regression analysis, classification models, and time series forecasting, the project identifies high-value customers, evaluates marketing channel effectiveness, and supports data-driven marketing strategy.

---

# Business Problems

- Identify customer segments with different purchasing behaviors.
- Predict high-value customers before future purchasing activity.
- Determine which marketing channels contribute most to revenue.
- Analyze long-term customer engagement trends.
